Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does WordBridge require an internet connection?
No! WordBridge is designed to work completely offline. The entire dictionary database is stored locally on your device. Internet is only used for displaying advertisements. All search and definition features work without any internet connection.
Q: How do I search for words?
Simply tap the search bar on the home screen and start typing. WordBridge will show matching results as you type. You can search for English words or Hindi words in Devanagari script.
Q: How do I save my favorite words?
When viewing a word definition, tap the heart icon to add it to your favorites. Access all your saved words anytime from the Favorites screen in the top navigation bar.
Q: Can I clear my search history?
Yes! Go to the History screen (accessible from the top navigation bar) and tap the trash icon to clear all search history. You can also swipe individual entries to remove them.
Q: Why is the app download size large?
The app contains a comprehensive offline database with over 1.4 million words and their complete definitions. This ensures you have full access to all dictionary content without needing an internet connection. The size is approximately 500MB.
Q: Is my data private?
Yes! All your search history and favorite words are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. The only data collection is through Google AdMob for advertising purposes. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Q: Where does the dictionary data come from?
All dictionary data is sourced from Wiktionary, a free multilingual dictionary project. The data is used under the Creative Commons CC BY-SA 3.0 license.
Q: Can I use WordBridge without ads?
Currently, WordBridge displays banner advertisements to support development and maintenance. The ads are non-intrusive and appear only at the bottom of certain screens. They require an internet connection to display.
Q: What platforms and versions are supported?
WordBridge supports iOS 12.0 or later and Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or later. The app is optimized for all iPhone, iPad, and Android smartphone/tablet devices running compatible OS versions.

Troubleshooting

Search not returning results

If you're searching for a word and not getting results:

  • Try searching for a more common word to verify the database is working
  • Check your spelling - the search is case-insensitive but spelling-sensitive
  • Try searching for just part of the word
  • Ensure you're using the correct language (English or Hindi)

App is slow or not responding

If the app feels slow:

  • The first launch may take a few seconds to load the database
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Ensure your device has sufficient free storage (at least 1GB recommended)
  • Restart your device if issues persist

Ads not displaying

Advertisements require an internet connection. If ads aren't showing:

  • Verify you have an active internet connection
  • Check that you haven't disabled ad tracking in your device Settings (Privacy settings on iOS/Android)
  • Note: Ads may not always be available based on your location
Note: All core dictionary features (search, definitions, favorites, history) work completely offline. Only advertisements require internet connectivity.
